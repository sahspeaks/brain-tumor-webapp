About Tumors and Types of Tumors

A tumor is an abnormal growth of cells that serves no purpose. A benign tumor is not cancerous and will not spread to other parts of the body. A malignant tumor is cancerous and can spread to nearby tissues and other parts of the body.

Types of Tumors:

  • Glioma: Gliomas are tumors that arise from glial cells in the brain or spine. They can be either benign or malignant.
  • Meningioma: Meningiomas are tumors that arise from the meninges, the protective membranes covering the brain and spinal cord. Most meningiomas are benign.
  • Pituitary Tumor: Pituitary tumors are tumors that arise from the pituitary gland, a small gland located at the base of the brain. Most pituitary tumors are benign.

To know more about brain tumors and their types, consult with a medical professional.
